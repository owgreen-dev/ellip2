- [T-014] `eval/pu_metrics.py`: the SCAR-safe reporting metrics (plan.md ┬ž7; RevClassify `final_test/{f1,prauc}`). **Four pure-numpy fns + `pu_metric_report` bundler**, label convention `1`=suspicious/positive `0`=licit/unlabeled (shared w/ Stage 2). **`pr_auc(scores,labels)`** = average precision `╬ú ╬öR┬ĚP` the sklearn way (NOT trapezoid): sort desc by score (mergesort, stable ties), `tps/fps=cumsum`, collapse to one point per distinct score via `boundaries=r_[where(diff(s))[0], n-1]`, `precision=tps/(tps+fps)`, `recall=tps/n_pos`, anchor at `(R=0,P=1)` then `sum(diff(recall)*precision[1:])`. It's a LOWER BOUND under SCAR (hidden positives in the unlabeled pool depress apparent precision). **`binary_f1`**=`2TP/(2TP+FP+FN)`, **`recall`**=`TP/(TP+FN)` (the one thing SCAR estimates unbiasedly), **`lee_liu_score`**=`recall┬▓/Pr(┼Ě=1)` (Lee&Liu 2003 lift ÔÇö rewards recall, penalises predicting-positive-indiscriminately via the over-ALL-data denom). Threshold-dependent three take **binary preds**; only pr_auc takes raw scores. `pu_metric_report(scores,labels,*,threshold=0.5)` thresholds `scores>=threshold` Ôćĺ dict keyed `COLUMNS=("prauc","f1","recall","lee_liu")`. **Degenerate guards (return 0.0, don't raise):** f1 when `2TP+FP+FN==0`, recall when no positives, lee_liu when no predicted positives; pr_auc DOES raise on zero positives or length mismatch. **`_as_binary`** rejects non-0/1 (`array_equal` after int64 cast catches floats like 0.5). **Clean hand values for tests:** scores`[.9,.8,.7,.6]`/labels`[1,0,1,0]`ÔćĺAP=**5/6**; yt`[1,0,1,0,1]`/yp`[1,0,1,1,0]` (TP2,FP1,FN1)Ôćĺf1=recall=**2/3**, lee_liu=`(4/9)/(3/5)`=**20/27**; selective`[1,1,0,0]`Ôćĺ2.0 > indiscriminate`[1,1,1,1]`Ôćĺ1.0 on yt`[1,1,0,0]` (proves the over-prediction penalty); tied `[.5,.5,.5,.5]`Ôćĺ0.5 (single boundary).
